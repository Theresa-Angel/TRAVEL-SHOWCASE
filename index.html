About Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu, located on the southeastern tip of India, is a land of ancient Dravidian culture, magnificent temples, stunning coastlines, and lush hill stations. With a history spanning over 2,000 years, it is home to some of India's most iconic landmarks.

From the UNESCO World Heritage temples of Mahabalipuram and Thanjavur to the mist-covered hills of Ooty and Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu offers an unparalleled diversity of experiences.

Best Season: Oct – Mar
Language: Tamil
Capital: Chennai
Climate: Tropical
